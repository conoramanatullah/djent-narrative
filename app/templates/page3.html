Genre Style IDK?

Djent - pronounced "duh-jent"

Djent is a genre of metal that emerged in the late 2010s onto the popular scene. Some people don't consider this a genre, they consider it a style. It honestly has a bit of both.

Common musical features include low tuned guitars and bouncy rhythms.

Bands like Periphery and Meshuggah paved the way for the sound of djent.

Djent is a genre

Djent can be described as a genre in many ways. The go-to explanation is that it is an entire community of musicians that share similar musical features. This can imply that djent is a full-blown genre like pop or punk.

Djent is a style

Djent can be considered a style because it is so varied in how musicians approach it. The style mainly consists of dropped tuned guitars, explosive drums, powerful vocals and musical virtuosity all around.
